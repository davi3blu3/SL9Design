Sandy Harned

designer, developer, project (and production) manager

I've had an amazing career as a theatre technician. I've been to all 50 states and over 15 countries. I've worked on the Vancouver Olympics and flown Peter Pan all over South Korea. I started experimenting with web design and development while coordinating content for a website mobile redesign, and I've been fascinated ever since.
My theatre background has given me unique skills that can be applicable and beneficial to many different fields. I'm a skilled project manager who knows how manage my time to meet crucial deadlines, because there is no deadline like Opening Night. Coordinating load-ins and figuring out flying scenarios is creative problem-solving wrought large. I've worked with designers and crews of varying levels of experience - not to mention varied temperaments - in high-stress situations. I'd love to put my skills to work for you.
